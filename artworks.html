Vehicles

Tank Nr.1

A slow tank, with a low fire frequency, but with an extremely strong gun, can drop mines.

Tank Nr.2

This tank is faster than Nr.1, has weaker gun, but a faster fire frequency. As Nr.1 it can drop mine.

Helicopter

The helicopter is faster than all ground vehicles, but it lacks a strong plating, so it can be easily shoot down. Weapons are a minigun and rockets.

Jeep

The jeep as basically no plating and only a week machine gun, but it is fast and the only vehicle that can capture the flag.

Hovercraft

Can travel over water and land. Weak gun, strong plating. Can't be destroyed by mines.

Trooper

Can transport a number of soldiers and release them. Weapons are two maschine guns + the weapons which the soldiers carry.

Artillery 1

Long Range Weapon Vehicle, can shoot gradates over a long distance.

Flak/MG

Extreme high fire frequenze, usefull against soldiers, planes and helicopters

Artillery 2

Same as Artillery 1, but shoots rockets. Longer Range and more powerfull.

Bomber

Might not be controllable directly by the player. Can only be destroyed by ground-to-air missles. Weapons: Lots of bombs, be used to destroy a basis.

UH-1 Huey

Rescue helicopter, might be able to capture the flag. Weapon is a maschine gun.

Ambulance

Rescue vehicle, can collect wounded soldiers

Soldier

There should be different kinds of soldiers, nothing specific at the moment. Soldiers should be controllable by the computer.
